Published: 2026-04-15 | Authors: William Sheu, Tommaso Treu, Martin Millon, Frédéric Dux, Devon Williams | Tags: astro-ph.CO, astro-ph.GA

Abstract

We present a blind time-delay cosmography measurement of the Hubble constant $H_0$ based on the quadruply imaged quasar SDSSJ1433+6007. Our analysis combines deep Hubble Space Telescope imaging, extended time-delay monitoring from the Wendelstein and Maidanak Observatories, and spatially resolved stellar kinematics from the Keck Cosmic Web Imager and Reionization Mapper. We build a robust lens model to reconstruct the mass distribution and high-signal-to-noise kinematic maps to break the mass-sheet degeneracy (MSD), explicitly accounting for the lens galaxy's oblateness, rotation, and anisotropy.
